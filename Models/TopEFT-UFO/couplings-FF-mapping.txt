### Aloha definitions

Momenta of particle N = P(mu,N)
This includes the momenta P(x,y). This first index is the lorentz index which should be contracted, while the second index refers to the particle ordering of the vertex.
E.g: P(-2,1)*P(-2,1) = p1(mu)*p1(mu)
       ^
       dummy index for sum
